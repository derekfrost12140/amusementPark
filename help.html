Welcome to Amusement Park Navigator. This application was created to help navigate Six Flags Over Texas.

To use this app, explore the Ride List Page or the Settings & Map pages. On the Ride List page, you can apply Sort & Filter options to find the best rides. Once you find a ride, simply click "Directions" and the map will take you to it.
